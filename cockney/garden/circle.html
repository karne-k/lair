Stone Circle

`Fish, Beetle, Frog, Sunner, Whalk (I think..)`
- Riven

Now this place is impressive, before you is a low hill with a flattened top. It's not that large - a few hundred strides across but is surmounted by seven huge standing stones - placed in a rough circle. The stones are a deep blue colour and are obviously foreign to this region. You get the feeling that they have been here a very long time. Indeed, they were almost certainly here long before your host moved in. You wonder who built this wonder - and how?!

After admiring the formation from a distance you walk forward and into the ring. Standing in the middle you feel a certain awe as you look around.

There doesn't seem to be much else you can do here. However, it is very peaceful and you wait a long while, before heading off to back to the lair.
