- Furry Code

You thank the dragon but explain you would like to spend some more time exploring alone. He nods understandingly and saya he looks forward to seeing you later. You thank him and as you leave, notice him curl back up in the sun and quickly return to his book. You get the impression your host is something of a bibliophile!

Leaving the garden, you pick a path pretty much at random and walk back into the forest. As you leave, you notice a scrap of paper pinned to a tree - it's a small, white label on a short length of string. Looks like it came lose from whatever it was tied to and has been pinned here for safe keeping. Somebody has printed the words 'Drink me' on it. Maybe this makes some sense to you and maybe it doesn't but either way you shake your head and continue on down the path.
