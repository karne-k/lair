There appears to a be a large purple dinosaur lying here. Or rather, the body of one as it appears to be somewhat dead. It's very bedraggled and covered in earth and broken branches. Walking over, you give it a prod with a long stick but there is no response - definitely dead. It seems to be made of some kind of cloth or felt - probably a good thing as there is no smell. Perhaps that's why it has been half buried - it's not as if any scavengers are going to want to eat it. You shrug and ignore the thing, it's probably safest.
