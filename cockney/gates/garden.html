ZigZag Path

`Do what thou wilt, and that
shall be the whole of the Law." `
- Aleister Crowley

You slowly walk down the long, sharply kinked path. It rather steep in places as it leads down the side of the crater wall but you reach the bottom safely. Here the path widens slightly and is thickly forested - trees overhang you from both sides and it's actually quite dark down here, despite the blazing sun. Rather cooler too, which is nice.

As you walk further, if anything the forest gets thicker and you find yourself wondering if it will ever end. Looking around you get a feeling of serenity and calm. You are alone here - free - with no one to judge or condemn you. Thinking on this, you realize why your host must choose to live here - perhaps he likes this feeling too?

Finally, the pathway opens out...
