Tower Roof

`In the Beginning, there was Darkness,
and the Darkness was without form and
void. And in addition to the Darkness,
there was also Me. And I moved upon
the face of the Darkness.
I saw that I was alone,
and this was not good.
And I determined to change this.
Let there be light!`
- The Bomb

It's but a few seconds effort and you've reached your goal. The roof of the tower is slightly convex, presumably to allow rain water to drain away and is surrounded by a low parapet. The view from up here is perhaps even more spectacular than from the gates, and you spend some minutes admiring it.

Eventually, you get bored with the view and explore the rest of the tower roof. You notice that the parapet on the southern side is much wider than elsewhere. Taking a closer look, you find than the stone has been engraved with a sort of pattern. It's rather worn and seems to be made up of a series of interlocking circle and lines, interspersed with tiny lettering. A map perhaps? If so, it could prove useful in finding your way around. You spend a few minutes studying the map - it's rather hard to read but you get a rough idea of the area. A number of things attract your attention...

There appears to be some sort of cave system marked on the map with a large area of gardens in frount of it. Perhaps you could find your host here? Alternativly, you might like to explore for a while longer - you have a feeling no one will object.
