Lets Fly

`Hey! What's this thing suddenly coming towards me very fast?
Very, very fast. So big and flat and round,it needs a big wide
sounding name like... ow... ound... round... ground! That's it!
That's a good name - ground! I wonder if it will be friends with me?`
- The Sperm Whale

Smiling nonchalantly, you launch yourself into a long circling glide, letting the air carry you outwards and down... Alternatively, you plummet like a bowl of petunias and you realise it would probably help if you opened your wings first! That's assuming you have wings of course. If you don't , well... I offer my sympathies and suggest you probably shouldn't have jumped in the first place, should you?

Either which way, you get an absolutely superb view on the way down. Below you, erratically tangled lines of hedges and overgrown flower beds, sprinkled with oddly neat lawns, suggest at some sort of garden. Some distance ahead is large circle of standing stones, set on a low hill or barrow. However, your attention is immediately attracted to huge temple like structure, apparently carved right into the eastern rock face. You head towards it and find an open space to land...
