Table

`Dreams surely are difficult,
confusing, and not everything
in them is brought to pass
for mankind.`
- Homer

This appears to be Karne's working table. Untidy stacks of paper litter the edges of the table - some have even been exiled to the floor. Even more papers have been screwed up and lobbed into untidy piles, you get the feeling that having a candle here would be a bad idea.

Out of curiosity you take a brief look at the top sheet in one of the stacks;

    De Novo Design of Novel Catalysts

    4 Helix Bundle Proteins

    1- Structural simplicity - stable - H bonding, X Ray?
    2- Use 4 identical sequences - synthesis easier
    3- 4 polyalanine alpha helixes....

You shudder and hurriedly drop the paper. Magic? Perhaps - and you decide you don't want to know. Looking around, you notice a slim blue book half pushed under the papers. Fishing it out and opening the cover you find it is filled with hand writing. It seems to be a book of stories, written by Karne himself. Interested, you find yourself a comfortable perch on a pile of grimiers and start reading..
