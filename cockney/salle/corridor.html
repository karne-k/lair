Corridors

`I left the world I know to watch a kid
try to put booties on a fucked up weasel.
Shoot me Roland before I breed.'
- Eddie

Karne notices you standing out in the corridor and hurries over. He seems a bit disappointed you didn't want to spend more time in his beloved library but is perfectly polite;

I see you found the way to the salle! A warrior at heart then, hrrm? That's not that surprising - what is, is how many are. Follow me then, I'll take you there.. and mind you don't lag behind, the Lair's a bit of a maze at the moment!

With that and a soft chuckle, the dragon rapidly disappears down the long, rather dimly lit corridor. If your sense of direction is correct, he's heading still deeper into the crater wall. Hurriedly you follow after..
