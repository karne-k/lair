Corridors

`Touch magic, pass it on.`
- Kathie

Karne doesn't seem to be in a particular hurry - but despite that, he's walking at quite a speed and you're having to push yourself to keep up. The passage seems to go on for ever and eventually you just shrug and slow down to a more comfortable pace, trusting the dragon to stay in sight. Karne doesn't seem to notice this and happily carries on walking.

A sudden shaft of brighter light catches your attention and you stop to investigate. Off to your left, a small, deep alcove has been cut into the passage wall. Normally hidden by a heavy tapestry, this has been pulled partly aside, letting you peer into a brightly lit chamber. This room has been painted a soft golden yellow and some sort of mural covers the far wall. A marble block supports a pair of candles, these fill the room with light and the fresh smell of bees' wax. Between the candles is a small gold vase, bearing single lily. This chamber is quiet and very peaceful - it would be a good place to rest a while. Glancing back down the tunnel - you see that Karne is all but out of sight- he doesn't seem to realise that he's lost you and he wouldn't notice if you ducked into the room. Curiosity wars with conscience, as you can guess this room is almost certainly private...
