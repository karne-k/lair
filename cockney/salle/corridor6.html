Corridors

`Meeple..'`
- Goldy

Dead end - you certainly can't go any further down this passage - it just stops at a half finished rock face. By the looks of it, this part of the lair is still being built and it could be dangerous. With an annoyed shrug, you turn round and head back the way you came.

After much wandering you are found by a rather amused feline servant who gives you directions to the salle. This time you have no problems getting there.
