The Workshop

This room is a right old mess! Despite being open and airy, it is cluttered with assorted 'objects' that cover every available surface. Obscurely shaped constructs of wood and metal jostle for space between strangely contorted glassware and a bizarre collection of clay models. There are papers everywhere! Karne looks rather embarrassed about the mess and attempts to clear you a little breathing space, before giving up in disgust...

Well, this is the workshop. This is where I do my work, when I can find the time. There's quite a lot of stuff here - some of it's concerned with building this place, some isn't. So if you're interested in building your own Lair, please feel free to look around and have a talk with me, I'm more than willing to advise. Most of the rest is to do with my experiments, I'm a bit of an alchemist in my spare time you see! My Laboratory is over there - thru' the door - go and take a look if you like. Try not to knock anything over though, some of the chemicals are rather dangerous... Through that door is my Library, though its rather muddled at the moment I'm afraid. If you're intrested in anything graphicy, you might like to try my Studio or the Gallery.

Oh! and watch out for Jooly by the way, she's a gift from a friend.
