The Bright Gallery

`Standing immobile throughout the
day, these vivid objects (with their
fantastic shadows on the wall behind
them shifting and elongating hour by
hour,) exuded a kind of darkness
for all their colour.`
- Hall of Bright Carvings

Karne smiles at your request and leads you out of the room, under an intricately carved archway. You don't get much of an opportunity to study the intricate carved stone walls as the dragon hurries down a corridor and up a short flight of stairs.

The room you enter is large and hexagonal. A vaulted ceiling supported with slender, stone columns soars above you. To your left and right the room extends outwards in long aisles or colonnades set with pillars. Before you, a deep alcove has been cut into the far wall. The walls and the great domed ceiling are all formed from a richly patterned, red marble.

As you look around you realise you are surrounded by colour, murals blaze from the walls - images of every shape and form bowl you over. For a few seconds you stand blinking, not sure where to look.

Karne is obviously amused by your reaction:

Welcome to my Gallery. This room is called the Hall of the Healers. To your left is the Aisle of Cats and to your right, that of Dragons. Before you is the Alcove. Please feel free to explore on your own. Come back to the study when you've finished and tell me what you think. I'll come and find you if you get lost!
