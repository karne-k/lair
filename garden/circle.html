Stone Circle

`Fish, Beetle, Frog, Sunner, Whalk (I think..)`
- Riven

Suddenly the trees thin out and you find yourself standing at the edge of a perfectly circular clearing. It isn't large, perhaps a couple of hundred paces across but its boundary is marked by seven huge standing stone. These appear to be made of a dark bluish granite - not a stone you recognise from this region - and each looms several times your height. They have a feeling of age and permanence; you suspect they were here long before your host moved in. You wonder who built this wondrous circle - and how?!

After admiring the formation from without, you step forward and enter the ring. Standing in the middle you feel a certain awe as you stare around you.

There are names here. Names of people I know, people I care for, people I Love. You know whom you are, which is fortunate since I've never found it easy to express myself. Your names are here as a record - know that you have made my life far, far the richer. With affection and gratitude;
