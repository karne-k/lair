The Emerald Forest

`Hooorrrrmmmmm`
- Ent

You wander in the cool shade of the forest. It's calm and peaceful, bird song the only thing you can hear. After a time, you find yourself in a small clearing and you bask for a while in the sunlight - you're acquiring a liking for this place.

As you look around, you notice there appears to a be a large purple dinosaur lying here. Or rather, the body of one - as it appears to be somewhat dead. It's very bedraggled - covered in earth and broken branches. Walking over, you give it a prod with a long stick but there is no response. Definitely dead. You notice its throats been torn open - must be some large predators around here, a thought that makes you a little nervous.

Oddly, the dinosaur seems to be made of some kind of cloth or felt - perhaps a good thing as there is no smell. It's not as if any scavengers are going to want to eat it. As you turn, a second flash of purple attracts your eye. There's a thing hiding under a nearby bush. It's large and gangly, with long purple fur. You stare at each other for a while, then the beast stick its tongue out at you and vanishes into the undergrowth.

You glance back at the corpse of the dino, then with a shiver decide to leave the area - fast. You could head onwards, take the first small path to your left or choose the second
