Getting Late

`That depends a great deal on
where you want to go.`
- C Cat

It's now getting very late! You're getting tired and you're sure there's something following you... You pick up the pace a little and eventually get back to the lair.

From the shadows, the huge cat blinks sleepily as it watches you escape - then nods dejectedly and with a slight grin, slowly disappears...
