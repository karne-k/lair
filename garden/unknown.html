Tomb Unknown

`‘Maybe I loved you, my mistress
and maybe I did not.
I wish I knew but then...
you never told me.
Goodbye mistress.`
- Karne

After a very long time, you suddenly find yourself in a small forest clearing. Looking up you see it's starting to get dark - you've been exploring for quite some time. As you walk forwards, you notice a standing stone in the middle of the clearing - it's not very large - about up to your chest and is shaped rather like a long thin pyramid.

Examining the pyramid, you get the impression of age, the dark granite is covered with a thin layer of green moss and it's some time before you notice the ankh carved into its surface.

You stand for a while - thinking - then decide that it's getting rather late and cold - time to go and find your host. That room he offered you sounds better by the minute and you could certainly use the rest. It's a very long walk back too - unless you can fly (in which case why have you walked this far?)
