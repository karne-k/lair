If you've visited the Lair before, then welcome back and have a look around - it's changed ]:=8) If not, you'll probably wondering where the hell you are and what this place is. Well you'll discover soon enough, I'm not going to say much except ask you to explore and see what you can find. I'll warn you now, the Lair is quite large!

If you're new here, you shouldn't use the nav bar down the side of the screen. It's there to allow previous visitors to get to places quickly - you'll miss a lot by using it. Also keep an eye on the 'status' bar at the bottom of your browser window - hints of where links go will be displayed down there. The site is built with an old text based adventure games in mind and is an ongoing project - so feel free to let me know what you think!

As for why I've built the Lair. This place is designed as a showcase for my art and fiction, as well as being an interesting home for Karne. It allows me to test and improve my html skills, giveing me something to work on when I'm not doing sites for other people. It's also somewhere I can show off what I do (and hopefully get jobs..!) Anyways - I hope you like it.
