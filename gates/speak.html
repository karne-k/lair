The Guardian

`Even so. It was my right and my
Night. Thou knowest, O Hathi.`
- Shere Khan

A little hesitantly, you try and strike up a conversation with the Guardian;

"Erm.. you mentioned your Master. Is he the one who owns this place?"

It's quite a while before the Guardian reacts and you wonder if it heard. Finally it blinks slowly and turns to answer you;

"Lord Karne 'th karnica is my Master and these are the gates to his Lair. You should know this, should you have reason to be here. Why do you not?
"Well, I just happened to be in the area, saw the gates and wondered who lived here."
"He does, as do I, as do others. Perhaps I should not have let you in."

Golden eyes hold your for several seconds, then the Guardian shakes it's head.

"But guests are welcome here, the Master likes to meet new people. If you intentions are honourable."
"I hope they are..."
"Ensure so, or I can't let you leave."

The cat stretches slowly, flashing crystal claws. It blinks again.

"He'll meet you in the garden."
"Urm - thanks. What's your name by the way."
"He calls me Shia. "

And with that, the cat shuts up and apparently goes to sleep. You are left wondering just how to get to the 'garden', it mentioned. Heading downwards seems the obvious solution so you head off down the path
