Waiting...

`Oh, let's just go ahead and
eat her. It'll save trouble`
- Yellow-Green Dragon

Time passes, rather slowly if truth be told. You spend it listening to the wind whistling through the trees and the occasional erratic burst of bird song. After about ten minutes, a soft growl from behind makes you spin round. You're just in time to see the Guardian stalking back through the gate to its original post. You get the distinct impression that it doesn't think much of you. More time passes...

It's starting to rain... this is distinctly boring you know?

After a while, you become aware of a strange fluttering, clanking sound. To your bemusement, a metallic winged creature appears and starts bobbing and flapping around you like a giant drunken bumblebee. To your greater astonishment, the creature appears to be entirely made of clockwork!

The little mechanical dragon dips and bounces expectantly before you for a few seconds, before zipping off at high velocity down the zigzag path. You are forced to dash after it in a rather undignified manner. At least it's stopped raining and the sun's out again...
