Watch Tower

`Dauntless the slug-horn to
my lips I set, And blew.'
- Child Roland to the
Dark Tower came.

You find yourself standing at the base of a squat round tower. It's built of cracked, bluish-gray stones - perhaps granite - roughly stacked one on another and is heavily overgrown. It stands in the middle of a natural clearing and the whole place has a feeling of age and slow decay. Walking round the base of the tower, you find a low arched entrance and a couple of window slits.

It's dark inside and it takes a few moments for your eyes to adjust. When they do, you can see a small circular chamber, walled and paved with stone. Your attention is attracted to a life sized mural on the far wall. It shows a dragoness lounging in a chair, behind her is an arched doorway, much like the one you've just passed through. Other than her, there is nothing else of interest in the room, nor is there any obvious way up the tower roof. It's cool and rather lonely in here.

Back outside, you look up at the top of the tower. It's about 30 feet above you and you can't see much of it. Climbing the tower doesn't really seem like a great idea, the cracks between the stones might give good purchase but the stones themselves are rather loose and you'd rather not injure yourself. About the only other way is to fly up - that's if you *can* of course. Otherwise, you get bored with looking at rock and head off back down the path.
