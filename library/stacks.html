The Stacks

`xxxxxx`
- Skallagrig

Wandering over, you take a closer look at the Stacks. They tower above you, row after row of tightly packed books and folios. Looking around, you estimate this library must hold thousands, if not tens of thousands of volumes. It's unexpectedly dark within the Stacks and you find the atmosphere oddly oppressive. They say that there's a magic inherent in books and that libraries act as depositories for this power. Around large collections likes this, strange things happens. Dusty magic. Here in the dark, you find yourself more willing to lay credence to these stories.

Despite your discomfort, your love of books leads you investigate the shelves. Rapidly you identify many of the volumes you know and love, also many more you have never heard of. You get the feeling your host as a deep, all abiding love for books.
