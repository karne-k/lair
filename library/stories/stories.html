`I am thy heart,`
- Sssasha

Karne 'th Karnica am I, The Shadow within thine Heart. I was named so by Linia 'na Tharn who bore me and by the stormdrake Greymeer, who sired me. I am of the line of Beloved Tithan, named Shadowbane and of those against which she fought and loved and died.

Both blood of Dragon and blood of Drake rage within me. My ancestors found their love and their desire in that war and so I walk this earth and play these skies. Healer and Hunter am I both, Mage and Warrior, for by those opposites that spawned me, so is dictated my wills, my desires, my loves and my very life.

Find now within thine hands, those stories of my life that I have seen fit to put to page. Read now and consider what I have done in my life and why, for here shalt I open up my heart unto you. Such things as these are dear to me and by mine actions shalt I be known, both to you and to myself. Read on.
