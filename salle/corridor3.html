Corridors

`Falling comets.
Best stay out of their way.
Old Dragon Lore.`
- Scoff

Back out in the corridor, you look around and wonder where the hell you are. The light from the room behind you spills out into the corridor but beyond this all is shadow. Taking a step forward, you wait until your eyes adapt to the light.

To your left the corridor stretches back to the study - you think you could probably find your way back there. Unfortunately you have no idea where the corridor to your right leads - presumably eventually to the salle where Karne is waiting for you and it would be polite to find him after all!
