screams the larynx of the merely magnificent`

After walking for some time, you stop in concern. This part of the Lair seems to be only half finished. The walls are rough stone, not polished to a glow like the rest of the tunnels. Several times you've had to scramble over piles of rubble and it seems to be getting worse..

You sure you're going the right way? - There's a small passage here - leading upwards. You could take that or just keep on going.
