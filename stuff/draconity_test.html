The 100 point draconity corruption test

version 1.0 by alpine@holly.colostate.edu - copy and distribute freely

 This test rates your relative draconity. The majority of these 100 questions assume that you are human (at least most of the time), have a draconic name, and have at least heard of dragons at some point in your life. So you won't have to wade through lots of questions asking if you've ever read a book on a dragon, seen a dragon picture, etc.

 This test is purely for fun and entertainment. If you do not feel that your score is relative to your draconity then ignore it, and feel good in the knowledge that if you belive you are a dragon then you have something no test can measure. - Dymus.

 Just for reference I found myself with a score of 56%. So I warn you now that the validity of the test isn't exactly real high, just fun.

 That's it. You can use your score to compare relative draconity with friends. To calculate your dragon purity score, count up your YES answers (UNchecked boxes) or press the button below if you can see it.
 The more dragon pure you are, the more like a dragon you are.

 ***Disclaimer: These questions do not necessarily represent what it means to be a dragon to all dragons. Some would disagree (including myself) that senseless destruction, the use of some words, and the ability to breathe fire are not all necessary draconic traits.

Score calculation and relative draconity

 0-15% pure - Sorry, you are still quite human.
 16-30% corrupt - You may have had a great-great-great-great-great grandfather who was a dragon
 31-45% pure - You have the heart of a dragon.
 46-60% pure - You are a dragon trapped in a human's body.
 61-85% pure - The only thing you are missing are scales.
 86-100% pure - Forget it, you are a dragon in mind and body.
