A strange room this - the walls are polished to a silvery glow but there is no apparsource of light. You realise, with a sudden shock you have no real idea how you got here, nor why. You remember another cave, in another place - but of the connection between there are here - nothing.

Confused, you shake your head to clear it and then take a good look around this small, stone chamber. For it is small, twice as tall as you are - it is almost perfectly spherical, you feel like you're trapped in a huge, silver ball. No visible entrances or exits mar the walls but as you look closer, studying your reflection, images seems to form within the wall... You try to focus on them but they twist and turn like fish in a pond, as if madly fleeing your gaze. It's only when you force yourself to concentrate on one area of the wall do things begine to stabilise..
